Why does liposomal delivery work so much better?

🔬
Liposomal Delivery Technology
50% higher bioavailability than standard Vitamin C [13]
+
[Liposome Diagram]

Liposomes are tiny phospholipid packets — the same material your cell membranes are made of. Each dose of Vitamin C is wrapped inside these packets, so your body recognizes and absorbs it directly into the bloodstream.

Standard Vitamin C must be broken down through the gut first. Liposomal bypasses that process entirely, delivering more Vitamin C, faster.

🌻
Sunflower Lecithin
Soy-free liposomal carrier — clean and effective
+
[Sunflower Lecithin Image]

Instead of soy lecithin (the most common liposomal carrier), we use sunflower lecithin — making it safe for those with soy sensitivities while delivering the same effective liposomal packaging.

Non-GMO, alcohol-free, and completely free from artificial additives.

🍊
Vitamin C (Sodium Ascorbate)
1,000mg per serving — immune, energy, and antioxidant support
+
[Vitamin C Image]

Sodium Ascorbate is a buffered, gentle form of Vitamin C that is easier on sensitive stomachs than ascorbic acid, while delivering the same powerful antioxidant, immune, and collagen-supporting benefits.

Combined with liposomal delivery, your body gets more of what you take, and gets it faster.
